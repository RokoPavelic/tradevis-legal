3. The service

Tradevis is an educational coaching tool for retail forex and CFD traders. Features include:

  • Strategy profile management (methodology, timeframes, risk preferences)

6. Connecting your broker

If you choose to connect a MetaTrader 4/5 broker account, you do so through MetaApi using a read-only investor password or, where you elect to place trades, a trading-enabled password. Tradevis stores broker connection metadata so the connection persists across sessions; passwords are handled by MetaApi and not retained by us in plaintext.

Trades you initiate in Tradevis are sent to your broker through MetaApi. Execution speed, slippage, and order rejection are governed by your broker. We are not responsible for broker-side issues including downtime, connection problems, or rejected orders.
